Jurassic Park III

Isla Sorna · 2001

Director: Joe Johnston Runtime: 92 min Rating: PG-13

Official Trailer

Synopsis

Dr. Alan Grant has become a vocal opponent of Jurassic Park, speaking out against the dangers of cloning dinosaurs. To fund his research, he agrees to guide a wealthy couple on an aerial tour of Isla Sorna. But the plane is diverted and crashes on the island — the couple has secretly hired him to find their missing son.

Stranded on the dinosaur-inhabited island, the group encounters terrifying new predators, including the massive Spinosaurus — a creature even larger and more aggressive than the T-Rex. They must survive raptors, pteranodons, and the relentless Spinosaurus to escape the island.

Behind the Scenes & Trivia

The Spinosaurus killing the T-Rex was controversial among fans. Spielberg wanted a new dinosaur to be the villain, but many felt the T-Rex deserved more respect.

The raptors in JP3 were designed with feather-like quills on their heads, reflecting new paleontological discoveries that raptors had feathers.

The plane crash sequence used a full-scale fuselage that could pivot and shake. The actors performed their own stunts.

The Spinosaurus sail was inspired by real fossils. Later discoveries confirmed Spinosaurus was semi-aquatic — ahead of its time!
