Featured Film: Jurassic Park (1993)

Billionaire John Hammond fulfils his dream of cloning dinosaurs from prehistoric DNA and builds a theme park on the remote island of Isla Nublar. Before the park opens, he invites a group of experts — palaeontologist Dr. Alan Grant, palaeobotanist Dr. Ellie Sattler, and chaos theorist Dr. Ian Malcolm — to verify its safety.

When a corrupt employee sabotages the island's security systems, the dinosaurs break free and the group must fight for survival against creatures that have not walked the Earth for 65 million years.
